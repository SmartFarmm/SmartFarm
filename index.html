Introduction

Welcome to our Smart Farm business plan. Our goal is to revolutionize poultry farming using smart technology to increase efficiency and productivity.

Farm Overview

Our farm currently houses 1500 big chickens and 1000 small chickens aged 8 weeks. We utilize state-of-the-art technology to monitor and manage the health and productivity of our flock.

Production Details

Daily egg production from big chickens ranges from 35 to 45 plates of eggs. The expected monthly income after 2-3 months of production is between 2.5% and 3%, with growth potential up to 5% monthly.

Financial Projections

We are seeking $100,000 USD for expansion to add another 5000 chickens, with a growth phase expected to last from 4 to 6 months. Initial net profit is projected to be between 2.5% to 3%, reaching up to 5%.
